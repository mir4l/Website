Features

Built for real friendships,
not finance bros.

GIVU is the debt tracker that feels like a social app — not a banking dashboard.

🧾
Receipt Aesthetic

Every friendship has its own receipt-style card. Thermal paper vibes, monospaced fonts, the whole deal.

⚡️
QR Instant Confirm

Scan a friend's QR code to instantly log a debt — no waiting for confirmation, no back-and-forth.

💱
Multi-Currency

JPY, EUR, USD, GBP, CHF and more. Debts in different currencies get converted to your base currency automatically.

🎨
Receipt Themes

Unlock premium receipt skins — Blueprint, Frost Glass, Ember, Tokyo Metro. Flex your style.

🔔
Activity Feed

Get notified when a friend adds a debt, confirms one, or logs a payment. Always in the loop.

🔒
Secure & Private

Firebase Authentication keeps your account safe. Only you and your friends see your debts.

How it works

Up and running
in 60 seconds.

1
Create your account

Sign up with a username and password — no email required for the simple flow. Or use Google if you prefer.

2
Add a friend

Share your QR code or send a link. When your friend scans it, you're connected instantly.

3
Log a debt

Enter the amount, pick who owes who, add a note. Send it for confirmation — or use Quick QR for instant logging.

✓
Stay on top of it

Each friendship has a receipt that shows exactly who owes what. Pay part, log a partial, and watch the balance update in real time.
