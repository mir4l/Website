How it works

Four steps to zero drama.

From sign-up to settled-up in minutes.

01

Create your account

Sign up with a username and password, or tap Sign in with Google for instant one-tap access.

02

Add a friend

Share your QR code or your unique friend link. When they scan it, you're instantly connected.

03

Log a debt

Enter the amount, pick who owes who, add a note. Send for confirmation or use Quick QR to log it instantly.

04

Stay on top of it

Live balance updates, partial payments, and a clear running receipt per friendship — always in sync.
